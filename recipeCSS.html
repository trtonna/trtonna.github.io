Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • butter
  • Milk
  • salt(optional)
  • bread (optional)

Directions

1. Plug in toaster and put two slices of bread inside, cook however you would like (optional, you can make the eggs without the bread). 2. Break open the eggs into a bowl. 3. Add milk into the eggs and whisk until it is a yellow mixture. 4. Turn on stove, and heat up a small amount of butter until it melts. 5. Pour the egg mixture onto the pan, and let it sit for about fifteen seconds. 6. Use your spatula to slowly scrape the eggs across the pan, to it starts to form solid chunks. 7. Keep doing this until there is no liquid left and it is all solid. 8. Turn off the stove and use your spatula to put it on a plate, or, if you made bread, put it on the bread. 9. Add a small amount of salt for flavor, and enjoy!
